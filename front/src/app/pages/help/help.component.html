Help & Support

Find answers to common questions or get in touch with our support team.

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" at the top of the page, enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. You can start using Kredia immediately.

What is my credit score based on?

Our AI analyzes your financial history, payment behavior, credit utilization, and other factors to calculate a comprehensive risk score.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use military-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and are ISO 27001 certified. Your security is our top priority.

What devices can I use?

Kredia works on any device with a web browser. We also have mobile apps for iOS and Android for the best on-the-go experience.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via email at support@kredia.com, through the in-app chat, or call +1 (555) 123-4567 weekdays 9-5 PT.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime without penalty. Just go to your account settings and click "Cancel Subscription." No questions asked.
