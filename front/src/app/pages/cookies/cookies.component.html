What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your browser or device. They help websites remember information about you, such as your login status or preferences. Cookies can be either:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Stored for a longer period or until you delete them

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in
  • Analytics: To understand how you use our Service
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Security: To prevent fraud and protect your account

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through your browser settings. You can refuse to accept cookies or delete cookies that have already been set. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Service.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third parties (such as analytics providers) to set cookies on our Service. These third parties have their own cookie policies. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.
